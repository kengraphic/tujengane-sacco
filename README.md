# TUJENGANE Growth Hub

## About TUJENGANE SACCO
TUJENGANE SACCO is a member-owned financial cooperative committed to building trust, transparency, and empowerment.  
We provide savings and loan services that help members achieve financial independence while fostering a strong sense of community.

## Vision
To be a trusted financial partner that uplifts members by promoting sustainable growth, innovation, and collaboration.

## Mission
- Encourage a culture of saving and responsible borrowing.  
- Provide accessible financial services tailored to members’ needs.  
- Build a transparent and inclusive platform where every member feels valued.  
- Celebrate milestones and achievements that reflect our collective progress.

## Community Impact
TUJENGANE SACCO is more than a financial institution — it is a community.  
Through collective contributions, we support members in education, business growth, and personal development.  
Our Growth Hub showcases milestones, success stories, and initiatives that strengthen our bond.

## Features of the Growth Hub
- **Member Registry**: A secure and transparent system for managing member profiles.  
- **Community Updates**: Highlighting events, achievements, and growth stories.  
- **Financial Tools**: Integration with M-Pesa for seamless contributions and transactions.  
- **Responsive Design**: Accessible across devices, ensuring every member can connect easily.

## Technologies Used
This platform is built with:
- Vite  
- TypeScript  
- React  
- shadcn-ui  
- Tailwind CSS  
- Supabase (for backend services)  
- Radix UI components  

## Contribution
TUJENGANE SACCO thrives on collaboration. Members and developers are welcome to contribute ideas, improvements, and features that strengthen our community hub.
